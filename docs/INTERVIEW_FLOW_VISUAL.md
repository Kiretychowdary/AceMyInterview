## 🎮 User Actions

### At Any Point:
- ✅ **View Progress**: Always visible in header
- ✅ **End Interview**: Available during breaks
- ✅ **Skip Break**: Continue without waiting
- ✅ **Track Time**: Live countdown visible

### Between Rounds:
- ✅ **Review Tips**: Quick preparation advice
- ✅ **Preview Next**: See what's coming
- ✅ **Take Break**: Automatic rest period
- ✅ **Quick Continue**: Skip if ready
